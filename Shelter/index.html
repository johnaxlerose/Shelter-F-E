About

Shelter Humanity ($SHELTER) is a ERC-20 token issued on the Binance Smart Chain on a mission to provide quality shelter to the less fortunate. Each time that Shelter is traded, 5% of the transaction is placed in our charity wallet.

Vision

We value actionable change in the world we live in through transparent charitable donations generated by the Shelter Humanity community. Our donation team uses funds from charity wallet to purchase quality living equipment that is distributed to the homeless worldwide

Transparency

We are placing an emphasis on showing where the donation wallet proceeds are going and sharing the stories of the people who these funds help. You will see lives changed at every step of our growth.

UniSwap

UniSwap is an Ethereum network based decentralized exchange or DEX for short. It is used for swapping ERC-20 tokens like Shelter. UniSwap will allow the community the ability to exchange their Cryptocurrency for Shelter.
