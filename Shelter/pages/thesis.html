The Crypto Project That Will Attempt To End Homelessness

Shelter is a humanitarian crypto project created by Humanitokens and backed by the blockchange group.

We believe blockchain has provided a market for creating a community based around charity that can eradicate great problems of resource distribution once and for all. Our tokenomics includes a built in 5% Charity on all trading action which funds a charity wallet.

Positive feedback is a process in which the end products of an action cause more of that action to occur in a feedback loop. This amplifies the original action.

Positive feedback loops in society win, they propagate over time and continue to expand until it would reach the full potential.

Unless a positive feedback loop for these global problems can be found, they will continue to be a problem.

To put this as a real world example imagine you must come up with 20 Billion grains of rice. But sadly you only have a million.

You also worked your whole life for that, and your kids are counting on it. You donate 900,000 grains because of your big heart…

Great just 19 Billion 999 Million and 100,000 grains to go. This is a direct attack, the grains of rice that go in do not produce any more rice. Thus, there’s no loop function, and no scale.

But let’s imagine a positive feedback loop based on a common fairy tale… You put 1 grain of rice in and that grain of rice doubles itself daily.

After 35 days you have created 34 Billion grains of rice.

The Shelter Effect: A real world look at the positive feedback loop.

Now picture this, with our tokenomics, every $2,000 of trading volume puts $100 of Charity fee back into helping.

That means for every $100 spent on charitable work, if we generate $2001 of market market volume we effectively have a positive feedback loop.

We have various methods planned in terms of creating positive media of the charitable work being done.

We believe showing the true tangible change, the tears of joy, and lives being changed being far and above effective in bringing this positive feedback loop into the world for the long term.
