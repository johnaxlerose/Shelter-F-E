F.A.Q

What are shelters tokenomics?

There is a 5% transaction fee that goes into a charitable wallet and a 3% transaction fee that goes back into providing liquidity.

What non-profit does shelter donate the charitable wallet to?

Shelter donates to Shelter Foundation. You can read more info about them on https://shelterfoundation.net

When is shelters launch date?

July 19th, 11AM PST 2021

How does the marketplace work?

Users can use shelter to incentivize other community members to do charitable acts of kindness for others.

What type of token shelter?

Shelter is a ERC20 token.

How can I buy shelter?

Currently, you can buy shelter using UniSwap. You can also watch our video on YouTube called how to buy shelter for a more in-depth process.
